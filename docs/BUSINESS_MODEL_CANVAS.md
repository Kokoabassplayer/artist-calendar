# Business Model Canvas (Draft)

## Value Propositions
- Poster-to-calendar pipeline that removes manual effort.
- Fast discovery of shows by artist, date, and location.
- Simple sharing for planning nights out with friends.

## Customer Segments
- Fans who discover shows via posters/social posts.
- Casual nightlife seekers who want quick plans.
- Organizers/venues (later) needing distribution.

## Channels
- Web PWA (primary).
- Social sharing links.
- Artist/venue referrals.

## Customer Relationships
- Self-serve discovery and planning.
- Personalized feeds based on follows.
- Optional reminders and alerts.

## Key Activities
- Ingest posters and normalize events.
- Maintain high-quality event data.
- Deliver search/discovery and calendar actions.

## Key Resources
- Poster ingestion pipeline.
- Event database + location normalization.
- UI/UX with liquid-glass design system.

## Key Partners
- Ticketing platforms (affiliate links).
- Venues/organizers for verified listings.
- Map providers for deep links.

## Revenue Streams (Post-MVP)
- Affiliate ticket revenue.
- Premium subscriptions (sync, alerts, saved searches).
- Promoted placements with clear labeling.
- API access for partners.

## Cost Structure
- Hosting (API + web).
- LLM inference (rate-limited on free tier).
- Email delivery for auth.
